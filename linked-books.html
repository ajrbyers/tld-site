The Lower Decks 2.001

The process

Four steps.

  • 1. Open the book in the Books plugin.
  • 2. Open the preprint in the repository.
  • 3. Link them from the book's Manage Linked Preprints page.
  • 4. The link shows up on both pages, the book and the preprint.

Step 4The link shows up

It works both ways.

You only link once, from the book side. The link then shows up on both pages:

  • On the book page, under Linked Items.
  • On the preprint page, under Linked Books.
