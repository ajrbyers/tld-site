Questions? Contact Andy Byers or Joan Kwaske.

02

Discord

While streaming the conference on Zoom, we also encourage you to join our Discord chats and voice channels to socialise with fellow remote attendees, join discussions, and submit online questions to presenters. It will also enable you to engage with Janeway's online community in the future.

You can join Discord at thelowerdecks.janeway.systems/discord. You'll find us under ‘The Lower Decks’.

You'll find:

  • A channel for general chat
  • A tech support channel
  • A panels channel where online attendees can respond to talks and ask questions for the speakers

03

Voice Channels

We also have the ‘Tavern’ voice channel, which will be used for breakout socials during lunch on both days, and on Friday after the keynote.

04

Staff & Welcome

All online attendees are invited to join Discord prior to the livestream. A member of our staff will welcome you and facilitate online greetings starting at TBC.

You'll see members of staff wearing Janeway t-shirts with our new branding both online and in the room.

05

T-Shirt Contest

Exclusively for online participants, we'll be running a contest with a Janeway t-shirt of your colour choice for the following categories:

  • Zaniest Discord contributor — send us pictures of your pets, reaction gifs, and more!
  • Most thoughtful question-not-a-comment for a panel speaker

Programme
