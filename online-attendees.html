Online Attendees

Welcome, online attendees

We are excited to welcome you to The Lower Decks 2.0, a Symposium on Janeway and Open Access Publishing, on Thursday 21 and Friday 22 May. The conference will be livestreamed on Zoom, with conversation and Q&A on Discord throughout the event.

Questions? Contact Andy Byers or Joan Kwaske.

02

Discord

While streaming the conference on Zoom, we also encourage you to join our Discord chats and voice channels to socialise with fellow remote attendees, join discussions, and submit online questions to presenters. It will also enable you to engage with Janeway's online community in the future.

You can join Discord at discord.gg/jkYPfj6q6N. You'll find us under ‘The Lower Decks’.

You'll find:

  • A channel for general chat
  • A tech support channel
  • A panels channel where online attendees can respond to talks and ask questions for the speakers

03

Voice Channels

We also have the ‘Tavern’ voice channel, which will be used for breakout socials during lunch on both days, and on Friday after the keynote.

04

Staff & Welcome

All online attendees are invited to join Discord prior to the livestream. A member of our staff will welcome you and facilitate online greetings starting at 09:15 (Dublin / IST) on both days, ahead of the 09:30 livestream.

05

T-Shirt Contest

To thank our online attendees for taking part, we're running a small contest across the two days. Two winners will each receive a Janeway t-shirt in the colour of their choice, posted to them after the event.

The categories:

  • Most spirited Discord contributor: for whoever brings the most energy to the chat (pet photos, reaction gifs, and good banter all count).
  • Best question-not-a-comment: for the sharpest, most thoughtful question put to a panel speaker.

Winners will be announced on Discord after the keynote on Friday. The OLH team will be judging; entry is automatic, just by joining in.
